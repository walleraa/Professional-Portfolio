About Me

Hey! I'm a software engineer at BNY's Pittsburgh office. I'm also a Univeristy of Michigan alumn (Go Blue!), with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the College of Engineering. I started learning how to code ever since my dad put a Raspberry Pi in front of me and had me learn Python by coding in Minecraft with it. I have since come a long way, with a couple years of experience under my belt in the form of internships with UKG and BNY (back when it was BNY Mellon) and almost a full-year of working full-time at BNY as a part of their Risk Engineering division.
